VJ-ATRON

A browser-based VJ station for learning the fundamentals of live video performance. Load your clips from your OneDrive folder and start mixing.

  • Dual deck video playback with independent controls
  • Crossfader — blend between Deck A and Deck B
  • Per-deck FX: brightness, contrast, saturation, speed, hue
  • Blend modes: Add, Screen, Multiply, Overlay & more
  • BPM Tap Tempo with beat flash indicator
  • Strobe & colour grade effects
  • Load video clips straight from your OneDrive folder
  • Full keyboard shortcut support

OneDrive: Click "LOAD FOLDER" in the library panel and navigate to your OneDrive Videos folder.
Supports MP4, WebM, MOV, OGG video formats.
